Senior Data Scientist
 Jakarta Permanent / Full Time
About The Company:
A pioneer in legal tech in Indonesia with primary focus is on providing online legal consultation services, connecting clients with professional advocates across various legal matters. 

Job Responsibilities: 
Analyze and leverage large-scale legal consultation data to improve platform efficiency, advocate-client matching, and user experience.
Develop data-driven models to optimize service delivery, ensuring seamless and efficient legal consultations.
Enhance search and recommendation systems to connect users with the most relevant legal professionals based on their needs.
Build and maintain data pipelines to support structured and unstructured legal case data for insights and analytics.
Collaborate with engineering and product teams to integrate data-driven enhancements into the platform, improving engagement and accessibility.
Research and apply advanced analytics techniques to refine user behavior analysis, fraud detection, and service optimization.
Monitor, evaluate, and continuously improve data-driven decision-making processes to ensure transparency, accuracy, and trust in the platform.
Key Requirements: 
Minimum 5 years of experience in data science roles, with intersection in the AI/ML field.
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, Master's degree in Behavioral Science would be a plus.
Strong proficiency in Python, SQL, including experience with data science libraries such as Pandas and NumPy.
Experience with machine learning frameworks, such as Scikit-learn.
Hands-on experience with data analysis and visualization tools, including Tableau and Power BI.
Strong analytical mindset with the ability to translate business challenges into data-driven solutions.
